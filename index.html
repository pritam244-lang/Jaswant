Meri sabse pyaari Payal,

Jab se tum meri zindagi mein aayi ho, tab se meri duniya poori ho gayi hai. Tumhari muskaan woh subah hai jiska mai roz intezar karta hoon, aur tumhari awaaz woh sangeet hai jo mere dil ko sukoon deti hai. Mai bahut khush-kismat hoon ki tum mere saath ho. Tum hi meri sabse badi khushi ho aur mera hamesha ka pyaar. Tumse kitna pyaar karta hoon, yeh shabd bayan nahi kar sakte.

Tumhara apna,

Jaswant
